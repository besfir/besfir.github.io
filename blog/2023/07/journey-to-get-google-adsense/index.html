Journey to get Google Adsense approval on a blog made with Nuxt

Besfir · July 15, 2023
Table of Contents
  • 🧐 The first review failed
  • 🔥 The second review also failed
  • ✅ Finally, It was approved in the third review

I applied for Google AdSense to boost my motivation to write something on my blog. I've connected to the Tistory-blog before, and I didn't think it would be this difficult because it was approved at once. It took about 12 days for each review, and it took a little more than a month to be notified of the final approval.

🧐 The first review failed

On June 2, when I first applied for AdSense on this blog, the number of articles was 8, and I wrote 16 more until I was notified of the results 12 days later, making a total of 24 articles. Previously, when it was approved by another blog at once, I applied when the number of articles was about 5 to 7, and I remembered that I wrote one or two more before it was approved, so I thought it would be approved without difficulty.

June 14th, I think it was around 15 o'clock. I was lying down and rolling around, and an email came, and as soon as I checked that the sender said Google Adsense, I ran to the computer. Thinking of installing an ad-sense module on Nuxt and setting it up, I smiled and accessed the ad-sense site, but the approval status was not ready.

When I checked the mail because it was strange, it was said that it was dropped due to the reason that without publisher-content or with low-value content. I've checked what I've written so far, but even if I think objectively, it wasn't worthless it to the point where I was embarrassed to put it out. Therefore, I thought the review was wrong, so I asked for a re-review without making any corrections.

After one night, I became cool, and I started looking for what the problem was because I thought if I left it like this, my re-review would fall. When I read the reason for the failed again, I saw a part that said, "without publisher-content".

In my case, when a 404 error occurs because there is no post I am looking for, I showed the error as it is on the page where the ad-sense is posted. From Google's point of view, there were only a few lines of text on the page where AdSense was published, so it seemed to have been a problem. Therefore, it was modified so that only pages with content, such as article list pages and posting pages, have ad-sense codes.

🔥 The second review also failed

On June 23, an email came at a similar time, and I read it relatively calmly because I failed once. To be honest, I didn't think it would failed even then. However, it failed again for the same reason.

If you wrote a blog platform, you can at least infer that there would be a problem with the number or content of posts, but when you created your own blog, you couldn't figure out what the problem was. I took my hand off the keyboard for a while and thought about it, and then I felt that the number of articles was not enough.

I wrote articles in Korean and English, and specified multiple languages using the hreflang tag, so I thought that only half of them were actually recognized. In addition, although it is not clear, there are rumors that it is more advantageous to get approval for AdSense if the credibility of the domain address is high, so I thought this would have an impact.

Taken together, we came to the conclusion that because we purchased the domain this time, the credibility of bestfir.xyz would be zero, and if so, it might have been dropped because we needed more posts. There were 22 articles written at this point, but as hypothesis that established previously, 12 articles were written if only the original was considered except for multilingualism. Up to here, I was nervous about something, so I asked to index all the posts I've written because I thought the number of posts indexed on the Google Search Console would also affect.

✅ Finally, It was approved in the third review

On July 5, six more articles were written and a total of 30 articles were written, and if the previous hypothesis was correct, the number of original articles excluding multilingual would probably have been recognized as 15. In the meantime, I have registered all my writings on the Google Search Console.

I got an email at a similar time when I received the review results before, and it was an email saying that advertisements could finally be posted. I went into the setting to post an advertisement with excitement, but I couldn't figure out how to set it up because I was using an automatic advertisement. I pressed this and that for about a day and tested what looked best on my blog, and now I was able to configure a layout that I liked to some extent.

In this way, I thought I would no longer have to suffer from Google AdSense, but I am trying to figure out what the problem is because I found a problem where advertisements are not posted intermittently.
