I bought my first perfume in my life.

Besfir ·Updated at June 14, 2023

I fell in love with the scent of hand cream that my sister gave me by chance, and I wanted this scent to come from me, not only my hands. So, when I searched, I sold perfume, and I applied right away because I could get free blotter of six signature products for just the delivery fee.

I decided in my mind that I should buy only one topknot cream until I receive the blotter. However, when I smelled it, I changed my mind to buy two types because I thought it would feel stuffy in the middle of summer, maybe because of the creamy scent.

I don't know much about perfume, so I'm going to organize it lightly and decide to buy it based on this.

Santal Cream

When I smelled it as a blotter, I felt it was a creamy and sweet woody scent and suede scent, so I felt it was a warm scent that felt heavy overall. However, when I sprayed it on the flesh after purchasing it, it was a little different from when I smelled it as a blotter. At first, it smelled clean and sweet, and as it went back, it smelled creamy, adding to the weight. I heard that there is also a fig scent, but I can't express it because I don't know the fig scent.

Due to its name, it seems to be compared a lot to Le Labo Santal 33, but it is similar only at the beginning, and this section is said to pass quickly. Rather, there is an opinion that it is similar to Diptyque Philosicos, so if you have time, it would be better to try it.

Gaiac Flower

I bought it and I'm using it, and I think I bought it really well. I bought it because I didn't have a choice because I excluded other perfumes from the purchase list, but honestly, when I smelled with blotter, I didn't find it very attractive because it was a bit pungent and heavy. I used it because I had to go out on a slightly hot day, but when I sprayed it on the flesh, it was very satisfying because it was cool and light and had an unexpected flower scent. It seems that you can feel the scent of roasted sweet potatoes as an aside story.

It is recommended for those who prefer cool flower scents such as Byredo Rose of No Man's Land or Zara Rose Petal Drops.

Gentle Night

"Oh? It's a very familiar scent." It was the first thought I felt after smelling it. I was glad that it smelled like vanilla and soft and sweet, similar to the skin I used when I was young, but I excluded it from the purchase list because it was not good enough to use as a perfume in my thought.

If you prefer a clean scent such as Le Labo Vanille 44, it will suit your taste, and it is recommended as a common perfume for men and women in their 20s.

Forget Me Not

Overall, the scent of basil was noticeable and clean and light, but it felt like a slightly wet grassy scent that felt like grass on a cloudy day. I didn't like spicy scents, so I excluded them from the purchase list, but after re-smelling them while writing this, I strangely want to smell them again.

What should I say, it's not a good scent because it's definitely not my taste, but it's so unique that I want to smell it again.

In The Shower

This scent is also unfamiliar, so I can't express it well. It seems similar to Forest, but it feels different, but it's smoky and cool, but it feels a little less pungent.

Personally, it smelled like smoked ham because it smelled a little salty with a smoky smell. I think it feels especially so when I put the blotter off my hand and smell it.

I got my first perfume of my life.
